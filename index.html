Frequently Asked Questions

What is SGPA?

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is the average grade point earned in one semester based on subject credits.

What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is calculated using all semester SGPAs and their credits.

Does UniGrade support JNTUK R23?

Yes. UniGrade follows the official JNTUK R23 grading and calculation system.

Is UniGrade free?

Yes. UniGrade is completely free for students.
